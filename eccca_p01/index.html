About Us

Established in 1976, the Essex County Chinese Canadian Association has served the Chinese community of Windsor and Essex County for over 30 years. Our vibrant community is involved in many community events such as the Carrousel of Nations, Dragonboat Racing, and the Annual Parade. We host many events throughout the year including our annual gala, golf tournament, and tea ceremony.
